01. About Me

Who are you?

click me to view my art portfolio

 Glad you asked. I'm Anshita and I have a passion for creating things. I'm a freelance artist and computer science student at Boston University. I love tackling challenges and making things that have tangible impacts.

 My interest in software engineering began unexpectedly with my Wordle obsession during quarantine. I had previously considered coding an arcane life-form, but the more I learned about it, the more I was enamored by the beauty, complexity and simplicity of it.

 Fast forward to today and I'm in my 2nd year of undergraduate studies and am currently working on a Cardano NFT project, Camel Club, as a developer. I want to learn more about building optimized and scalable programs and aspire to a career that will allow me to channel a facet of my creativity through code.
