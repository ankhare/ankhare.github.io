Objective

What are we trying to do?

Our main objective is to develop a hotel booking website that provides customers with a user-friendly platform to easily search, compare, and book hotel rooms across various locations.

In effort to tap into the growing trend of experiential travel, where customers are looking for unique and exciting travel experiences that go beyond just a place to sleep that are still affordable. By offering a user-friendly platform that highlights "deals that are out of this world," we are looking to attract customers who are looking for something different and exciting in their travels.

Scope

Deliverables

  • Conduct market research and generate a report that details information about the hotel industry and the competitive landscape of existing hotel booking websites.
  • Identify key features and functionalities that users look for in a hotel booking website and determine how they can be incorporated into the new website.
  • Develop a comprehensive plan for the website's design, outlining the layout, color scheme, and overall aesthetic of the website.
  • Create a detailed development plan that includes the programming languages, software, and technology stack to be used for building the website.
  • Test and launch the website, ensuring that it is user-friendly, secure, and fully functional.
  • Provide ongoing maintenance and support for the website to ensure that it remains up-to-date, secure, and optimized for user experience.

Assumptions

  • Responsive website design compatible with various devices (desktop, mobile, and tablet) and screen sizes.
  • Secure user authentication and registration system.
  • Integration of a reliable payment gateway for secure online transactions.
  • Robust search and filtering system for easy navigation and hotel discovery.
  • Interactive map displaying hotel locations and nearby attractions.
  • Real-time guest reviews and ratings from popular review platforms through API integration.
  • Live chat and customer support system for seamless communication with guests.
  • Content management system (CMS) for easy updating and maintenance of hotel information, images, and promotions.
  • Advanced analytics and reporting tools to monitor website performance, user behavior, and booking trends.

Limits and Exclusions

  • Website content creation, such as articles or blog posts, is not included in the website development scope.
  • Ongoing website maintenance and updates beyond the initial development phase will require a separate agreement.
  • The website development team is not responsible for any legal or regulatory compliance issues related to the hotel business.
  • Third-party costs, such as API subscription fees or payment gateway charges, are not included in the website development budget and are the responsibility of the client.
  • The website development team is not responsible for the performance of third-party services or any issues arising from their use.
