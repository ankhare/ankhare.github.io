Meet Dr. Khare

Founder and General Surgeon at Spectrum Surgical PC.

As a board-certified general surgeon, I have extensive training and experience in a wide range of surgical procedures. I prioritize open communication and collaboration with my patients to ensure they are fully informed and involved in their treatment plan.

I believe that you can learn a lot about a person by knowing their values, these are mine:

 Trust.  It's not easy to entrust your health or the health of your loved ones to just anyone. I believe in earning every patients trust by taking the time to know understand each individual and their unique needs.

 Empathy.  I know how difficult it can be to navigate though complex medical system when you are also worried about your health, outcomes and bills. Our team's goal is to make the process as easy as we can for you.

 Care.  It is my personal mission to provide unbiased, non-judgemental, patient centered care to every patient.
